The Bride

Like every love story, it always starts with one moment that makes your heart skips a beat and your world turns around. Strangely enough, our story began with a simple Facebook message from Sushank to Mohini that changed our life forever. Our relationship then filled with long distance calls and virtual commitment between Shirpur and Pune for a good 3 years.

Oh yeah, did we mention that our first time meeting each other physically was 3 months after we committed into the relationship? Yes it was! And one funny story, the first thing that came out from Sushank when we met for the first time at Prakasha Temple was "You Look Much More Beautiful Than You Look On Social Network". And then we laughed together. That was definitely an ice breaking moment!

Fast forward to January 2015, Sushank proposed to Mohini and luckily she said... 'YES!'. This wedding will definitely be one of the most important moments in our life and we want to share the memories with people that we care the most.
